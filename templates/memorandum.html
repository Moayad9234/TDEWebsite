Memorandum of Agreement

Purpose

The Memorandum of Agreement (MoA) outlines the expectations, responsibilities, and schedule for a student participating in an internship for TDE 407. It must be signed by the student, the site supervisor, and the university faculty coordinator.

Internship Objectives

The internship provides an opportunity for students to participate in professional operations that enhance their knowledge in graphic communications and design-related fields.

Responsibilities of the Student Intern

  • Follow company work hours, policies, and conduct standards
  • Maintain confidentiality of information
  • Demonstrate responsibility and professionalism in tasks
  • Develop self-awareness of work-related values and behaviors
  • Apply academic learning in a real-world setting
  • Engage with learning opportunities offered through the internship
  • Submit progress reports and time logs to the faculty coordinator

Internship Schedule

The student is expected to work an average of [XX] hours per week for a total of 300 hours over the semester. The final schedule is determined in agreement with the site supervisor.

Project Documentation

Students must describe internship projects, their connection to TDE/GC practices, and confirm their work with the supervisor.

Agreement Signatures

This document must include signatures from:

  • The student
  • The site supervisor
  • The NC State faculty coordinator

Note: This is a structured version of the full MoA document. Refer to your advisor or internship coordinator for the official form.
